Welcome to AI Study Buddy

  1. Pin the extension. Click the puzzle icon in Chrome’s toolbar, then pin AI Study Buddy.
  2. Start LM Studio. Load your model (e.g. google/gemma-4-e4b) and start the server on http://localhost:1234.
  3. Set your goal. Click the extension icon, enter what you want to study, choose a duration, and start.
  4. Stay accountable. The AI will check in periodically, nudge you if you drift, and softly block distracting tabs.

Everything stays local by default. Browsing data is only sent to your own LM Studio server.
