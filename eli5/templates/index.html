Ethical Considerations & Media Literacy

This tool uses AI to simplify news. Understanding its limitations is essential for responsible use.

Reliability & Autonomy

Every summary displays confidence and modification scores. High modification means you may be missing nuance. The AI can make mistakes — always verify with original sources.

Critical Thinking

Simplified news can remove vocabulary you need, omit historical context, and discourage deep analysis. Use this as a starting point, not a replacement for reading full articles.

Bias & Echo Chambers

AI inherits biases from its training data. Summaries may favor dominant viewpoints. Article recommendations can create filter bubbles. Actively seek diverse sources.

Economic Impact

Reading only summaries reduces traffic to original news sites, potentially defunding journalism. When possible, visit the original source — every click supports the reporters who did the work.

Corporate & Government Influence

AI models reflect the values of their creators. Summaries may subtly push corporate or political agendas. Be aware of who controls the AI that filters your understanding of the world.

Future of Journalism

As AI writes, summarizes, and curates news, the role of human journalists is at risk. A world of fully AI-generated news lacks accountability, on-the-ground reporting, and the human judgment that holds power to account.
