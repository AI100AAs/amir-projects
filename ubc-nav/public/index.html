      Privacy & how this works

      "Scenic", "quiet" and "calming" are subjective. This app's idea of a nice route comes from preset path attributes and a model's reading of your words — it can be wrong, and it reflects whoever's preferences shaped it. Treat suggestions as a starting point, not ground truth.

      No account, no database, no movement log. Your destination and request are sent to the server only to interpret the constraint; routing runs in your browser, and shareable links encode state in the URL, not on a server.
